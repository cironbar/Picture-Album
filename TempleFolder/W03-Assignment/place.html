History

Nigeria, known as the "Giant of Africa," has a long history of ancient kingdoms such as Benin and Oyo. It gained independence from Britain in 1960 and has since grown into one of Africa’s most influential nations.

Culture

Nigeria is home to over 250 ethnic groups including Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa. Its culture is vibrant, celebrated through festivals, Nollywood films, Afrobeats music, and delicious foods such as jollof rice and suya.
